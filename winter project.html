    Get the speed of toy die-cast cars!

    I'm sure all of us remember playing with toy cars at some point in our childhood, and maybe you even kept playing till today. There's something inherently satisfying about seeing a scale car model zooming through a race track of your own design.

    However, It'd be a lot more satisfying if we had a tangible number of exactly how fast they were actually going, right?

    Thats where this project comes into play. Using a few electronic components, we can accurately measure the car's velocity on a given piece of track.

    Construction

    This circuit works by Measuring the distance we want to cover and timing how long it takes the car to cross this distance via 2 passage sensors.

    This data gives us enough to calcuate the cars speed via simple formula. This number is then shown on a nearby LED screen for easy viewing.

    The construction of this mechanism is fairly simple, and putting it together is farily enjoyable.
